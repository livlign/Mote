# Mote

One word. One day. The whole world.

---

Every day, somewhere around eight billion people wake up feeling *something*. Most of it goes unsaid.

Mote asks for one word. Just one. Whatever's loudest in your head right now — *tired*, *hopeful*, *Tuesday*, *rain*. You type it, you're done. You can't say more even if you wanted to.

Then you see everyone else's. The words that lots of people picked grow large. The lonely ones stay small but they're still there. It's a snapshot of a planet's interior, set in type.

At midnight UTC the page empties and the day starts again. Yesterday is gone. There's no archive, no profile, no streak, no score. Just today, in a word.

---

Mote will live at **[mote.day](https://mote.day)** — coming soon.

It is a single HTML file and a Postgres table. That's the whole thing.
