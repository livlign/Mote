One word per person, per day. The world's words, set big.

Each day at 00:00 UTC, the canvas resets. Everyone gets one word — whatever's on your mind, in your language of feeling. The more people who say a word, the larger it lands.

It's a quiet snapshot of a planet's mood, drawn one mote at a time.
